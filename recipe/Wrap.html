Wrap

Description

To make a Wrap you need some ingredient:

  • Raw Wrap
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese
  • Salad
  • Mayonaise
  • Ham
  1. Flatten the Wrap
  2. Add ham, cheese, salad and some tomato slices
  3. Add Mayonaise
  4. Fold the Wrap
  5. Put the Wrap in the pan
  6. Wait 8 Minutes
  7. Your Wrap is finished!
