Pizza

Description

To make a pizza you need some ingredient:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Salami
  1. Flatten the Pizza dough
  2. Add Tomato Sauce on the dough and distribute it
  3. Add Cheese
  4. Put some slices of Salami on top
  5. Put the Pizza in the oven
  6. Wait 45 Minutes
  7. Your Pizza is finished!
